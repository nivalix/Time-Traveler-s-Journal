Gymnopédie No. 1

The Composer ▼
Erik Satie was a French composer known for his minimalist and atmospheric music. "Gymnopédie No. 1" is one of his most famous works.
What’s the song about ▼
A gentle ripple of melancholy and grace, Gymnopédie No. 1 captures the delicate soul of La Belle Époque. With its slow tempo and dreamlike atmosphere, this piano piece evokes a quiet Parisian evening—where gaslamps flicker and thoughts drift like soft clouds over Montmartre.
