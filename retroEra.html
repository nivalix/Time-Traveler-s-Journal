Recommendation of the day: "Vienna" by Billy Joel

The Singer ▼
Billy Joel is an American Singer, Songwriter, and Pianist. His music is an irrefutable proof that piano is a rock and roll instrument.
What’s the song about ▼
Vienna was written by Joel and released as a part of his 1977 album The Stranger. It's trying to convey that there is so much life to live in old age, and that it's okay to stop and take a breath for a while.
